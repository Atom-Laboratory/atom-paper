# Suggested Research Topics for Robotics Projects

## 🔹 Robot 1: Arm with Computer Vision

### 🧠 Idea 1 — Modular Low-Cost Robotics Architecture
- **Theme:** Modular architecture for robotic manipulators with embedded computer vision
- **Contribution:** Vision/decision/control separation, inter-module communication, latency comparison
- **Experiment:** Total time (image → action), pick-and-place success rate

### ⚙️ Idea 2 — Precision vs. Cost in Vision-Controlled Manipulators
- **Theme:** Evaluating precision and performance of low-cost vision-controlled robotic arms
- **Contribution:** Functional robotics without expensive hardware, real metrics
- **Experiment:** Positioning error (cm), success rate (%), calibration impact

### 👁️ Idea 3 — Vision Robustness in Real Environments
- **Theme:** Impact of lighting variations on object detection in low-cost robotic systems
- **Contribution:** Literature-grounded problem, simple vs. robust solutions comparison
- **Experiment:** Different lighting conditions, algorithm comparison

### 🤖 Idea 4 — Real-Time Vision Pipeline for Control
- **Theme:** Optimized computer vision pipeline for real-time embedded robotic control
- **Contribution:** Performance focus (FPS, latency), optimization techniques
- **Experiment:** FPS vs. resolution, latency vs. precision

## 🔹 Robot 2: ATOM Chess (Academically Strongest)

### ♟️ Idea 5 — Chess Robot with Vision + Internal State
- **Theme:** Hybrid architecture for autonomous chess execution with embedded vision
- **Contribution:** Move detection over full board reconstruction, FEN state maintenance
- **Experiment:** Incremental vs. complete reconstruction, latency, error rate

### ⚡ Idea 6 — Edge AI in Real Robotics
- **Theme:** Embedded computer vision performance under hardware constraints
- **Contribution:** Raspberry vs ESP32, local inference
- **Experiment:** Processing restrictions and edge computing trade-offs

### 🧠 Idea 7 — Vision + AI + Physical Control Integration
- **Theme:** Integrating visual perception, decision-making, and kinematic control
- **Contribution:** Complete autonomous system (rare in undergraduate projects)
- **Experiment:** Full pipeline execution, total decision-to-movement time

### 📉 Idea 8 — Precision vs. Latency Trade-offs
- **Theme:** Analyzing precision-latency trade-offs in embedded robotic systems
- **Contribution:** When to reduce resolution? Simplify models?
- **Experiment:** Real research methodology

### 🔬 Idea 9 — Custom vs. Generic Datasets
- **Theme:** Impact of personalized datasets on object detection in controlled environments
- **Contribution:** Your chess board dataset vs. generic dataset
- **Experiment:** Accuracy, training time, generalization
